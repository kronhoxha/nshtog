Our story

More than
great coffee

N'Shtog is a cozy corner of Pristina where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee meets warm hospitality. Every cup is crafted with care, every guest welcomed like a regular.

We partner with Buondi Craft to bring you beans sourced from Vietnam, Brazil, and Colombia — roasted dark, brewed right.
